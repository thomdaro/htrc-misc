The President met at the White House
with:
-Vice President Mondale, Secretary
of State Cyrus R. Vance, Hamilton
Jordan, Assistant to the President,
and Dr. Brzezinski;
-Mr. Moore;

sylvania;
-Vice President Mondale, Secretary
Vance, Dr. Brzezinski, and Mr. Jor-
dan;
-a group of editors and news directors
(transcript will be printed next
week).
The President attended the final ses-
sion of a conference of members of the
Federal Regional Councils in Room 450
of the Old Executive Office Building.
The President declared a major disas-
ter for the State of Massachusetts as a re-
sult of coastal flooding, beginning about
February 6, which caused extensive pub-
lic and private property damage.
The President left the White House for
a weekend stay at Camp David, Md.

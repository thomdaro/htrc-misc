Administration of Jimmy Carter, 1978
January 20
The President met at the White House
with:
-Dr. Brzezinski;
-Senator Frank Church of Idaho;
-Thomas J. Watson, Jr., chairman of
the executive committee of IBM
Corp.;
-members of the President's Commis-
sion on White House Fellowships.
The President declared an emergency
for the State of Arkansas because of the
impact of tornadoes which struck Cross,
St. Francis, and Woodruff Counties on
January 7.
The President left the White House for
a weekend trip to Georgia.

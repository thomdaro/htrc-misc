Special Message to the Congress Reporting on a Budget Deferral.
September 14, 1976
To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I report a net
increase of $11.1 million in the amount previously deferred for the Social
Security Administration's Limitation on construction account.
The details of the revised deferral are contained in the attached report.
The White House,
September 14, 1976.
GERALD R. FORD

Dear Mr. Chairman:

tem of Preferences (GSP) the Trade Policy Staff Committee (TPSC) con-

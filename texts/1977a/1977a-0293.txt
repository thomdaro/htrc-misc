The President met at the White House
with:
-Dr. Brzezinski;

with:
-Dr. Brzezinski;
-the National Security Council;
-Mr. Lance and Charles L. Schultze,
Chairman of the Council of Eco-
nomic Advisers;
-El Hadg Omar Bongo, President of

The White House announced that the
President has declared an emergency for
the States of New Mexico and Michigan
because of the impact of a drought, and
for the State of North Carolina because
of the impact of drought and freezing.

Administration of Jimmy Carter, 1977
The President today announced the
three persons who will represent the
executive branch on the Conference on
Security and Cooperation in Europe.
They are:
PATRICIA DERIAN, Coordinator for Human
Rights and Humanitarian Affairs, Depart-
ment of State;
DAVID MCGIFFERT, Assistant Secretary of De-
fense for International Security Affairs; and
FRANK WEIL, Assistant Secretary of Commerce
for Domestic and International Business.

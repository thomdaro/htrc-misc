The President declared a major disas-
ter for the State of Maryland as a result
of ice conditions in the Chesapeake Bay
region beginning about January 1, 1977.
The President declared an emergency
for the State of Virginia as a result of ice
conditions in the Chesapeake Bay region
and the Atlantic Coast of Virginia begin-
ning about January 1, 1977, which caused
extensive public and private property
damage.

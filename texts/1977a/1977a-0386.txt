Administration of Jimmy Carter, 1977
March 9
The President met at the White House
with:
-Dr. Brzezinski;
-a group of Senators and Congress-
men to discuss foreign assistance
legislation;
-Pak Tong-chin, Foreign Minister of
the Republic of Korea, to discuss se-
curity issues, including the proposed
withdrawal of American ground
forces from Korea, and the human
rights situation.
The President announced the designa-
tion of Edward D. Re as Chief Judge of
the United States Customs Court.
The President acknowledged the retire-
ment of William H. Becker as a United
States District Judge for the Western Dis-
trict of Missouri, effective February 8.
Judge Becker will assume the status of
Senior Judge and continue to be available
for assignment.

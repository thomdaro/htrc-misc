December
21 The President and Mrs. Nixon greeted
Members of Congress and their families
attending a Christmas reception at the
White House.

James R. Schlesinger and the Joint Chiefs

December
26 Following his meeting at the White House
with Secretary of State Henry A. Kissinger,
Soviet Ambassador A. F. Dobrynin joined
with Secretary Kissinger in a meeting with
the President in the Oval Office.
28 The President and Mrs. Nixon attended the
wedding of Maj. Gen. Walter R. Tkach,
USAF, Physician to the President, and
Cheryle Ann Gaillard in La Jolla, Calif.

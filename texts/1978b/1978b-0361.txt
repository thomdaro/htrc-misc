September 17
The President met in the morning with
members of the U.S. delegation.
The President held morning and after-
noon meetings with President Sadat. Also
attending the afternoon meeting was
Secretary of State Cyrus R. Vance.
The President met in the afternoon
with Prime Minister Begin.
In the evening, the President, accom-
panied by President Sadat and Prime
Minister Begin, returned to the White
House.
September 18
The President met at the White House
with Members of Congress to discuss the
results of the Camp David meetings.
The President announced the appoint-
ment of Robert L. Herbst, Assistant Sec-
retary of the Interior for Fish and Wild-
life, as a Commissioner of the United
States Section of the Great Lakes Fishery
Commission.

Statement on Signing H.R. 13745 Into Law.
October 5, 1978
Today I am signing into law H.R.
13745, the Sikes Act Amendments of
1978.
On July 10, 1978, I vetoed an earlier
version of this legislation which would
have more than doubled the 1978 author-
ized funding level under the act and would
have required the Secretaries of the In-
terior, Agriculture, and Defense to explain
Presidential budget requests for less than
the amount authorized.

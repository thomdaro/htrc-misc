Nomination of Nicholas A. Veliotes.
August 8, 1978
The President announced today his in-
tention to nominate Nicholas A. Veliotes,
of California, as Ambassador to the
Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan. He would
succeed Thomas R. Pickering, who is be-
ing appointed to another position.

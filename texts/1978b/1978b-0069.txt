The White House announced that
President Carter's mother, Lillian
Carter, will visit the Sahel region of West
Africa as an emissary of the President
from July 17 to August 2. Her journey
will express the President's deep interest
in the problem of world hunger, the dis-
parity between rich and poor, and the
role of women in the development proc-
ess. On her way to the Sahel, Mrs. Carter
will stop in Paris, to meet with repre-
sentatives of the Club du Sahel at the
Organization for Economic Cooperation
and Development, and Rome, to receive
the Ceres Medal from the Director-
General of the Food and Agriculture Or-
ganization of the United Nations.

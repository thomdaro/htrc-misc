The President has declared a major
disaster for the State of Texas as a result
of severe storms and flooding, beginning
about August 1, which caused extensive
public and private property damage.
The White House announced that ap-
plications are now available for the White
House Fellowship program for 1979-80.
The program is open to all U.S. citizens,
except those in civilian Federal positions.
Each fellow works for a Cabinet officer or
senior member of the White House staff,
and participates in a program including
seminars with top Government officials,
leading scholars, and journalists who deal
with the National Government. Applica-
tion forms and additional information can
be obtained by sending a postcard to the
President's Commission on White House
Fellowships, Washington, D.C. 20415.

246 Letter Resigning the Office of President of the
United States. August 9, 1974
Dear Mr. Secretary:
I hereby resign the Office of President
of the United States.
Sincerely,
RICHARD NIXON
[The Honorable Henry A. Kissinger, The Sec-
retary of State, Washington, D.C. 20520]
NOTE: The President's letter of resignation was
delivered to Secretary Kissinger in his White
House office at 11:35 a.m., August 9, by As-
sistant to the President Alexander M. Haig, Jr.

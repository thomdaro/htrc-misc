National Commission on
Social Security
Nomination of Elizabeth Duskin To Be a
Member. January 15, 1979
The President today announced that
he will nominate Elizabeth Duskin, of
Chevy Chase, Md., to be a member of
the National Commission on Social
Security.
Duskin, 43, is director of research for
the National Council of Senior Citizens.
She has also served as an economic con-
sultant to the Federal Council on Aging,
where she prepared congressionally man-
dated studies. Her professional back-
ground has emphasized programs and pol-
icies affecting U.S. income distribution,
particularly social and private insurance
policies, income-conditioned programs,
and health-related financing mechanisms.

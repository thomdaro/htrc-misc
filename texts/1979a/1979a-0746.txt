The President declared a major dis-
aster for the State of Texas as a result of
severe storms, tornadoes, and flooding,
beginning on or about April 18, which
caused extensive damage to private prop-
erty.
The President declared a major disaster
for the State of North Dakota as a result
of severe storms, snowmelt, and flooding,
beginning on or about April 11, which
caused extensive public and private prop-
erty damage.

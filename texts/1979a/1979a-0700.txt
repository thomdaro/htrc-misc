Nomination of Lawrence A. Pezzullo.
April 24, 1979
The President today announced that
he will nominate Lawrence A. Pezzullo,
of Bethesda, Md., to be Ambassador Ex-
traordinary and Plenipotentiary of the
United States to Nicaragua. He would
replace Maurice Solaun, who is being
assigned to the State Department.
Pezzullo is currently Ambassador to
Uruguay.
He was born May 3, 1926, in New
York City. He received a B.A. from Co-
lumbia University in 1951. He served in
the U.S. Army from 1944 to 1946.

viously chairman of the department of
preventive medicine of the University of
Maryland and is an internationally known
epidemiologist.

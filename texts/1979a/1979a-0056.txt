Nomination of W. Beverly Carter, Jr.
January 15, 1979
The President today announced that he
will nominate W. Beverly Carter, Jr., of
Philadelphia, Pa., to be Ambassador at
Large. He would have overall responsi-
bility for liaison with State and local
governments for the State Department.
Carter, 57, is currently Ambassador to
Liberia. He joined the Foreign Service in
1965 and served as a public affairs offi-
cer in Nairobi and Lagos. From 1969 to
1972, he was Deputy Assistant Secretary
of State for African Affairs.

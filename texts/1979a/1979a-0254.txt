The White House released manifests
listing passengers who have flown on
White House authorized military aircraft
from July 1 through December 31, 1978.
The lists were sent to Representative Jack
Brooks, chairman of the House Commit-
tee on Government Operations, and were
also made available for inspection by the
press.
As required by Public Law 95-426, the
President transmitted to the Speaker of
the House of Representatives and to the
chairman of the Senate Foreign Relations
Committee reports on "International
Journalistic Freedom," "International
Communications Policy," and "U.S.
Policy Toward Cuba." He also trans-
mitted to the chairman of the Senate
Committee on Commerce, Science, and
Transportation the report on "Interna-
tional Communications Policy."
In the evening, the President partici-
pated in a foreign affairs briefing for a
group of Senators.

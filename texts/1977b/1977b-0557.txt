September 28
The President met at the White House
with:
-Mr. Aaron;
-Mrs. Carter, for lunch;
-members of the Commission on Fed-
eral Paperwork, who presented the
Commission's final report.
The President attended a briefing on
the Panama Canal treaties, given for
State officials and business and political
leaders from Arizona, Colorado, and Del-
aware in the State Dining Room.
The President announced that he will
nominate Carolyn R. Payton to be Di-
rector of the Peace Corps. Ms. Payton
was nominated to be Associate Director of
ACTION on September 7.

Delaware River Basin Commission
Appointment of Sherman W. Tribbitt as
Alternate Federal Member. December 5, 1977
The President today announced that
he has appointed Sherman W. Tribbitt,
of Delaware, to be alternate Federal
member of the Delaware River Basin
Commission. He replaces Thomas F.
Schweigert, resigned.
Tribbitt was born November 9, 1922,
in Easton, Md. He is a graduate of Bea-
com College. He owned and operated the
Odessa Supply Co. in Odessa, Del., for 28
years.

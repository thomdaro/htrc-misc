Nomination of Robert N. Smith To Be an
Assistant Director. July 15, 1977
The President today announced that he
will nominate Robert N. Smith, of
Bloomfield Hills, Mich., to be an As-
sistant Director (Office of Community
Action) of the Community Services Ad-
ministration.
Smith was born July 13, 1944, in
Detroit, Mich. He received a B.A. in 1967
from the University of Michigan and a
J.D. in 1970 from the University of
Michigan Law School.

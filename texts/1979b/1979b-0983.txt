November 6
The White House announced that the
President met with senior foreign policy
advisers this morning to discuss the situ-
ation in Iran. The United States has been
given assurances by the authorities in Iran
that the safety and well-being of Ameri-
cans will be protected. The United States
expects that these assurances will be hon-
ored. The White House further an-
nounced that in the meantime, the ad-
ministration is doing everything possible
to secure the release of the Embassy staff.
The administration does not consider
public comment on these efforts to be ap-
propriate or helpful while they are in
progress.

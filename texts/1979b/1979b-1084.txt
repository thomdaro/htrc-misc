In reaction to the terrorist attack on a
Navy schoolbus near San Juan, P.R.,
which resulted in the death of two U.S.
sailors and the injury of several others,
the White House announced, on behalf of
the President, that this was a despicable
act of murder; that the full resources of
the Federal Bureau of Investigation and
the Department of Justice will be available
as needed to help identify and bring to
justice those responsible for a senseless act
of terrorism; and that such actions are not
consistent with the beliefs of the vast ma-
jority of Puerto Ricans, whatever their
political views.

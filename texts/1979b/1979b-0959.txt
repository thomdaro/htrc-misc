October 30
The President met at the White House
with:
-Zbigniew Brzezinski, Assistant to the
President for National Security
Affairs;

the President on Aging;
-members of the Ad Hoc Leadership

member of the Advisory Commission on
Intergovernmental Relations for a 2-year
term.

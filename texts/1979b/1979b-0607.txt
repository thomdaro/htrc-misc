SECTION 6. Abolition
The East-West Foreign Trade Board
established under section 411 of the Trade
Act of 1974 (19 U.S.C. 2441) is abol-
ished.
SECTION 7. Responsibility of the Secre-
tary of State
Nothing in this reorganization plan is
intended to derogate from the responsibil-
ity of the Secretary of State for advising
the President on foreign policy matters,
including the foreign policy aspects of in-
ternational trade and trade-related
matters.

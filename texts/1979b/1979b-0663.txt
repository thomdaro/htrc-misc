1-103. Agency for International
Development.
(a) The Director shall continue within
IDCA the Agency for International De-
velopment, heretofore established in the
Department of State.
(b) The Agency for International De-
velopment shall be headed by an Admin-
istrator appointed pursuant to section
624(a) of the Act.
(c) The officers provided for in section
624(a) of the Act shall serve in the
Agency for International Development.
1-104. Office of Small Business. The
Office of Small Business provided for in
section 602(b) of the Act shall be in the
Agency for International Development.
